Smart OCR FAQ

What is the difference between Free and Smart OCR?

Free OCR runs on your device for one page and works best on clean printed text, with no layout or tables. Smart OCR uses GPT-4o for much higher accuracy, keeps layout as Markdown, extracts tables, reads handwriting and many languages, and handles up to 30 pages.

How much does Smart OCR cost?

2 credits per page. New accounts get 10 free credits, and you can buy credit packs on the pricing page. The free on-device OCR costs nothing.

Can it read Korean and handwriting?

Smart OCR (GPT-4o) handles Korean, mixed languages, and handwriting well. The free on-device option is weaker on those — use Smart OCR for best results.

What is a searchable PDF?

A PDF that keeps the original page images but adds a hidden text layer, so you can select and search the text. Smart OCR can export one.
