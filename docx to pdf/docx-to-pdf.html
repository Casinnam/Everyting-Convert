Office & Documents

Convert Word documents to PDF

Sending a Word file risks layout shifts, font swaps, and accidental edits on the recipient's computer. Converting your DOCX to PDF freezes the document exactly as you designed it, so it looks identical for everyone and cannot be changed by mistake. It is ideal for resumes, contracts, letters, and reports.

Best results

  • Use standard fonts for consistent results
  • Keep to text-focused documents
  • Review the preview before sharing

Common fixes

  • Unusual fonts may substitute, so use common fonts
  • Complex layouts may shift slightly
  • Large images increase file size

Private by design

Your document is converted locally in your browser and is never uploaded.
