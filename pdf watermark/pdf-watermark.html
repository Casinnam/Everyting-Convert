PDF & Documents

Stamp a text watermark across your PDF

A watermark is a simple way to mark a PDF as confidential, draft, or yours before you share it. This tool overlays your own text, such as “CONFIDENTIAL” or a company name, across every page, and lets you control the wording, size, angle, and opacity so it is visible without hiding the content. It all runs in your browser, so the document never leaves your device.

Best results

  • Use a diagonal angle so the mark is hard to crop out
  • Lower the opacity so text underneath stays readable
  • Keep the wording short, like DRAFT or CONFIDENTIAL

Common fixes

  • Watermark hides the text? Lower the opacity
  • Too small to notice? Increase the size
  • Need it on only some pages? Split the PDF first, then watermark

Private by design

Watermarking runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your PDF is never uploaded, stored, or shared, so confidential files stay on your device.
