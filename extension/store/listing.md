## Single purpose (for the review form)
Provides quick access to EverythingConvert's free online file-conversion tools through a toolbar launcher and a right-click menu. It opens the matching tool on everythingconvert.com — no file data is processed or stored by the extension itself.

## Detailed description
Convert files without hunting for the right website. EverythingConvert puts 20+ free online tools one click away — straight from your browser toolbar.

⚡ One-click launcher
Click the toolbar icon for an instant menu of popular tools — Image Converter, PDF to Word, Merge PDF, Compress PDF, PDF to JPG, QR Code, and more. Just start typing to search.

🖱️ Right-click to convert
Found an image on a page? Right-click it and choose "Convert with EverythingConvert" to jump straight into the image converter. Same for PDF and file links.

🌐 Runs in your browser
There's nothing to install and no desktop software. Conversions happen on the web, fast and securely, on any computer.

🆓 Free to use
All standard conversions are free. Optional Pro and AI tools are available on the site when you need them.

🔒 Privacy-friendly
The extension doesn't read your browsing, doesn't collect personal data, and doesn't upload your files. It simply opens the right tool for you.

Supported tools include: PDF to Word, PDF to Excel, PDF to JPG, PDF to PowerPoint, Merge / Compress / Split PDF, Image Converter (JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC), Image to PDF, Video & Audio converters, QR Code generator, and more.

Open EverythingConvert: https://www.everythingconvert.com/

## Privacy practices (store form answers)
- Does it collect user data? No.
- Permissions used: `contextMenus` (to add the right-click menu). The extension only opens tabs to everythingconvert.com; it does not access page content, browsing history, or files.
- Data sold to third parties? No.
- Data used for purposes unrelated to the single purpose? No.
