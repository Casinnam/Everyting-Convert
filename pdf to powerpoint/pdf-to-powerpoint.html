Frequently asked questions

Is the PDF to PowerPoint converter free?

Yes. Image-slide conversion is free for everyday use. AI editable-text slides use credits, and Pro removes daily limits.

Will each page become its own slide?

Yes. Every PDF page is placed on its own slide, so the deck mirrors the document page by page.

Can I edit the text after converting?

Choose AI text slides to get selectable, editable text. Image slides keep the exact look but are pictures, so the text is not editable.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

Image-slide conversion runs fully in your browser. AI text slides send only the extracted page text, never the original file.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. It runs in any modern browser on phones, tablets, and computers, though large PDFs are easier on a desktop.
